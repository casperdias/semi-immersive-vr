Image Files for IPAL Stage Viewer

Add your screenshots here with these filenames:

- stage1.png  (Equalization Tank / Bak Ekualisasi)

- stage3.png  (Biological Process / Proses Biologis)

Images will be displayed in the viewer panel (220px height) with object-fit: cover.

Recommended image size: 288px × 220px or wider for best quality.
